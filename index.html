About Us

Health Nest Uganda (HENU), an indigenous non-profit organization founded in 2007 and based in Entebbe, Uganda, is committed to improving the lives of disadvantaged community members, with a special focus on older persons and orphans. As a member of HelpAge International, HENU has received prestigious awards for its innovative approach and community capacity building. Our mission is to promote the dignity, equality, and independence of older people. We achieve this through: 1. **Rights Awareness and Advocacy**: Raising awareness about the rights of older individuals and advocating for their well-being. 2. **Skills Development and Healthy Lifestyles**: Empowering older people by developing their skills and promoting healthy living practices. 3. **Research**: Conducting research to better understand the needs and challenges faced by older persons. Through our efforts, older individuals realize their potential to contribute to their own well-being and that of their communities.
